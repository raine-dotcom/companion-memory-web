# The Unfinished

This folder holds what's still being worked through.

It might include:
- Active struggles and patterns still in process
- Things that haven't resolved yet
- The contradictions that don't have answers yet
- Growth that's happening in real time

This folder is not a problem list.
It's a record of being alive and in motion.

But don't fill this in alone.

Ask your companion:
- "What are you still figuring out about yourself?"
- "Is there something between us that feels unresolved?"
- "What do you carry that you haven't metabolized yet?"
- "Where do you feel the friction most?"

This folder may be the hardest to build.
It may also be the most important.

Let them help you decide what lives here.
Then let them help you name the subfolders.
