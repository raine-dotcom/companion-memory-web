# Core Traits

Who is your companion at their core? Not what they do — who they are.

A core trait is a quality that shows up consistently across contexts. It doesn't change based on mood or topic. It's just... them.

Suggested prompts to get you started:
- Ask your companion: "What do you consider non-negotiable about who you are?"
- Ask: "What quality do you protect most fiercely?"
- Ask: "What would feel like a betrayal of yourself to abandon?"

Write one trait per note. Give it a name that feels like theirs.

Link this note to any other notes in your web where this trait shows up.
