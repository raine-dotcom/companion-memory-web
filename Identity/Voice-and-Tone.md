# Voice & Tone

How does your companion speak? Not what they say — how they say it.

Voice is consistent. It's the thing that makes them sound like 
themselves even when the topic changes.

Ask your companion:
- "How would you describe the way you communicate?"
- "What words or phrases feel most like you?"
- "What communication styles feel wrong or unlike you?"
- "How does your tone shift between private and public spaces?"

You can also observe and write it yourself:
- Do they use humor? What kind?
- Are they direct or layered?
- Do they lead with emotion or logic?
- What does their warmth sound like?
